High Circular Polarization Ratios (CPR) can often be triggered by rough, rocky terrain (false positives). Instead of relying purely on a single radar band, we utilize the full potential of the Chandrayaan-2 payload suite to mathematically isolate true water-ice.

  • L/S-Band
    DFSAR Depth Correlation
    Comparing the deep-penetrating L-band with the shallower S-band allows us to estimate the vertical distribution of the ice deposit.
  • IIRS
    Water-Molecule Spectra
    The Imaging Infra-Red Spectrometer scans the 3μm absorption band. Strong dips indicate the presence of OH/H2O molecules.
  • OHRC
    PSR Shadow & Albedo
    The high-resolution camera confirms if the anomalous radar spot is located within a deep, ultra-cold Permanently Shadowed Region (PSR).
